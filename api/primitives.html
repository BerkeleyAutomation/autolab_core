__init__(min_pt, max_pt, frame)¶

Initialize a box.

Parameters:
  • min_pt (numpy.ndarray of float) – The minimum x, y, and (optionally) z points.
  • max_pt (numpy.ndarray of float) – The maximum x, y, and (optionally) z points.
  • frame (str) – The frame in which this box is placed.
Raises:
